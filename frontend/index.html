What's a
Nifty Noodle?

NiftyNoodles are inspired by garden eels (or 'sea noodles', as we like to call them). They spend the majority of their time partially embedded in the sea floor, instinctively burying themselves in the sand every time someone comes close to them. Only a small colony of 9,999 of these modest creatures were brave enough to venture out of the ocean and embed themselves into the Ethereum blockchain to become NiftyNoodles. By adopting a NiftyNoodle, you'll help ensure their survival in this strange new digital habitat.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I get a NiftyNoodle NFT?

The official public sale will he held on 6th of June, 2022. Presale will occur 48 hours before and will only be available to members on the Good Noodle list.

How do I join the Good Noodle list?

You need to be on the Good Noodle list in order to access the presale and get 50% off. To find out more, check out the discord!

How many can I mint?

There will be a maximum of 10 NiftyNoodles that can be minted per TX.

How much will it cost to mint?

One NiftyNoodle will cost 0.025 Eth to mint.

What can I do with a NiftyNoodle?

NiftyNoodles aren't your traditional PFP collection. This multi-format interactive NFT will serve as your cross-platform access card to a range of metaverse platforms.

What is a dPad? (It's free ethereal estate!)

dPads are the ultimate homes for NFTs. These ethereal estates not only make for beautiful banner images that compliment your PFP on social profiles, but they will also display NFTs in your connected wallet. Lucky noodle holders (Noodlers) will receive subaquatic dPads in Reef Street, Sea-Shill-Ville, Bikini Boulevard, and Coral Country - It's free ethereal estate!
